Trip Tips & Reminders

🌍
America the Beautiful Pass
$80 annual pass covers all national parks. Pays for itself: Rainier ($30) + Crater Lake ($30) + Redwood. Buy at first park entrance.
🌧
Pack Layers
Rain jacket is essential. Warm layers for Rainier & Crater Lake. Windbreaker for the coast. Water shoes for Fern Canyon.
📱
Download Offline Maps
Cell service gaps near Mt. Rainier, rural Oregon coast, and Crater Lake. Save Google Maps offline before each drive day.
💰
Bring Cash
Redwood NP / Fern Canyon day-use fee is $12 cash only. No cards accepted at Gold Bluffs Beach kiosk.
❄
Check Road Status
Morning of Day 2: nps.gov/mora for Rainier. Day 5: nps.gov/crla for Crater Lake. Trillium Lake road may still have snow.
💵
No Sales Tax!
Oregon has no sales tax. Menu prices = what you pay (plus tip). Stock up on shopping in Portland or Eugene!
